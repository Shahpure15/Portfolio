Traffic Buddy - Violation Reporting Platform

Traffic Buddy is a collaborative project with the DCP Office Pune, designed to empower citizens to report traffic violations through a WhatsApp chatbot. The platform streamlines the process of reporting and addressing violations, ensuring accountability and smoother traffic management.

The backend is built using Node.js, Express.js, and Twilio for chatbot integration, while the frontend for officer operations is developed using React.js. The platform features a 14-division login system, mapping to Pune's traffic divisions, and a central dashboard for comprehensive report management. Division-specific logins allow officers to access and manage reports relevant to their jurisdiction.

Traffic Buddy aims to enhance traffic law enforcement and citizen participation, fostering safer roads and a more organized traffic system.
